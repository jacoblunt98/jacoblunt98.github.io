Intro

Growing up in Iowa does not come with a lot of perks. For one you have to know how to deal with the weather, the "people", and the lack of activities. Once you figure that out, there isn't much else to learn. Just wait till your 18 so you can move to another state. Everyone does it. Before I get carried away, this is a little summary about my life growing up in Iowa.

My Life

My name is Jacob Blunt and I was born and raised in Southern Iowa. I have 2 siblings and 4 pets. I am going to college and am enrolled in the Interactive Media Program at Indian Hills Community College. I plan on going to a 4-year college after I graduate from IHCC. I am always trying to make myself a better person whether that is physically or mentally.
